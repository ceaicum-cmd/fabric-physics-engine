# Failure Modes & Realism Escalation

## Critical Failure Modes and Fixes

1. **Uniform smoothness**
   - Fix: localized tension concentration, asymmetrical compression behavior, non-uniform wrinkle spacing.

2. **Skin-fused clothing**
   - Fix: preserve garment thickness, layered depth, edge compression, and physical separation from anatomy.

3. **Missing force chains**
   - Fix: directional stress transfer between anchoring points through seam-guided propagation.

4. **Over-idealized compression clothing**
   - Fix: subtle elastic instability, micro-compression ripples, pressure gradients.

5. **Missing material identity**
   - Fix: keep deformation consistent with stiffness, elasticity, friction, thickness, and weight.

## Advanced Physics Upgrades
- anisotropic stretch behavior
- contact deformation under interaction
- gradual tension decay across distance
- subtle micro-asymmetry in wrinkles and seam pressure

## Realism Escalation Strategy
1. Increase gravity references.
2. Add seam realism.
3. Add compression language.
4. Increase fold specificity.
5. Reinforce material thickness.
6. Reapply anti-painted-on rules.

## Intensity Mapping
- **L1 (Subtle):** portraits, beauty, close-up fashion edits.
- **L2 (Enhanced):** stylized realism, moderate reshaping, editorial full-body.
- **L3 (Extreme):** major anatomy edits, difficult garments, skin-tight outfits, layered complexity, dynamic scenes.
